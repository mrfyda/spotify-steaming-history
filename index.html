How to get your data

Spotify

  1. Go to spotify.com/account/privacy
  2. Request the Extended streaming history (your full lifetime history; takes up to 30 days and is worth the wait) or the basic Account data (last year only, arrives in a few days)
  3. Spotify emails you a download link. Drop the zip here as-is, no need to unpack it

Apple Music

  1. Go to privacy.apple.com and choose Request a copy of your data
  2. Select Apple Media Services information (it arrives within a few days)
  3. Drop the zip here as-is — the play-activity file inside has your full history

Exports can take a while to arrive. so this page doesn't get lost in the meantime.
