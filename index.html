How to get your data

  1. Go to spotify.com/account/privacy
  2. Request the Extended streaming history (full lifetime history — takes up to 30 days, worth it) or the basic Account data (last year only, arrives in a few days)
  3. Spotify emails you a download link — drop the zip here as-is, no need to unpack it
